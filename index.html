Free Tech Consulting. Real Experience.

We are a student-run consulting group that provides free technology consulting to small businesses, nonprofits, and student organizations. In return, our consultants gain real-world, hands-on experience by working on live projects that deliver measurable impact for our partners.

Our model creates a win-win: organizations receive high-quality technical support, and students build practical consulting and implementation experience.

What We Offer

Data Solutions

Turn your data into actionable insights. We help organizations make sense of their data through analysis, visualization, and proper database systems. Whether you're drowning in spreadsheets, need to understand trends, or want automated reporting, we build custom solutions that fit your needs.

Salesforce for Nonprofit Organizations

Implement and configure Salesforce's free Nonprofit Success Pack to manage your donors, volunteers, and programs. Salesforce offers 10 free licenses to qualifying nonprofits - we help you take advantage of this powerful tool without the enterprise price tag.

Digital Customer Experience Audit & Optimization

Make it easy for customers to find you, trust you, and do business with you. We audit your digital presence from a customer's perspective and implement fixes that directly impact how people discover and interact with your organization online.
